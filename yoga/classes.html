    Yoga Classes

    Gentle Hatha Yoga

      Intended for beginner and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this

      60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alighnment and posture),

      pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

    Vinyasa Yoga

      Although designed for intermediate to advance students beginners are welcome to sample this

      60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement - you will inhale and exhale as

      you flow energetically through yoga poses.

    Restorative Yoga

      This 90 minutes class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair

      or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.

      This practice is can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
