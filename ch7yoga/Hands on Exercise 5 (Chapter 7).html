My Favorite Hobby: Soccer

Materials for Soccer

  • A Soccer Ball
  • 22 playeers. 11 on each team
  • Field to play
  • Referee
  • Goal Post
  • Flags

How to Play Soccer

  1. Step 1: One team start with the ball in the middle and pass to their team mate when the referee blows the whistle.
  2. Step 2: When one of the team score the ball in the goal post, they score a point and the team start with the ball in the middle.
  3. Step 3: The process is repeated for 40 minutes then they take a break and continue the game for another 40 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Which ever team with the highest score wins.
